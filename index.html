About Me

civil engineer & project manager

Ayawavi Attisso is a civil engineer with 12 years’ working experience in building, surveys, designing, and control. Currently she serves as the technical adviser for infrastructures in the international NGO: Handicap International, Togo-Benin Program. She is focused on renovating several maternity buildings in public health centers, promoting access and facilities to people with disabilities. She is also an activist for the education and health of girls, helping women and youth to fulfill their dreams and achieve their goals through education, training and mentoring. Ayawavi holds a master’s degree in Civil Engineering and Infrastructures from Fondation 2iE in Ouagadougou, Burkina Faso. Upon completion of the Mandela Washington Fellowship, Ayawavi plans to continue her work in promoting public health standards according to infrastructure and facilities to disabled persons, promoting women working in the civil engineering and mining sectors as well as encouraging girls and women to choose science and technology studies.
